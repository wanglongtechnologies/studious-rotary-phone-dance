Rules & Regulations

Application

Terms

  1. Contestants are only allowed to submit ONE (1) entry per event.
  2. Contestants can participate in multiple events but must adhere to the rules.
  3. Age will be calculated according to the birthdate from the participant’s Identification Card.
  4. Participants participating in Grade A events are not allowed to participate in Grade C events.
  5. The adjudicators’ decision shall be final and no correspondence will be entertained.

Dress Code

Male

  1. Plain black trousers and plain white long sleeves shirt with black tie or bow tie.
  2. Rolled up sleeves are strictly not allowed, black narrow belt with small buckle.
  3. Black Latin or ballroom shoes worn with black socks.

Female

  1. Simple dress of same colour and material.
  2. No decorations such as sequins, lace, rhinestones, beads, feathers, fringes, etc.
  3. No plunging or revealing front neckline or backline lower than top of armpit.
  4. No spaghetti strap, off shoulder and/or cut outs.
  5. Skirts must be of one length hem throughout.
  6. Latin dress must be at least knee length.
  7. Ballroom dress length must not be more than 10cm above top of sock line
  8. Only light make-up is permitted but false eyes lashes are strictly not allowed.
  9. No accessories (e.g. armbands, wristbands, chokers and/or headbands) are allowed.
  10. Only black hair band is allowed.
  11. Dancing shoes with blocked heels only (maximum 3.5cm high) must be worn with white ankle socks.

Music

In all events, the selected music will be provided by the organizer. All contestants must strictly perform his/her dance with the music accordingly. If the submission uses the incorrect event’s music, the submission will be disqualified.

Video Submission

Guidelines & Requirements

  1. Video length shall not exceed 3 minutes 30 seconds.
  2. Video must be uploaded in MP4 format (.mp4).
  3. Video size shall not exceed 1GB.
  4. This is strictly a dance contest. Hence additional video editing is strictly NOT allowed.
  5. Video must be taken in full-body length to ensure good visual of his / her choreography
  6. All contestants must strictly perform his / her dance with the respective music provided by the organizer. If video submitted using the INCORRECT EVENTS / CATEGORY MUSIC will be disqualified.
  7. The organizer reserves the rights to disqualify entries that do not strictly follow the video requirements.

Awards
